Why this course?

AI is changing how we work and learn. This course bridges the gap between hype and practical utility.

ETC 599: AI for Master Learners uses a project-based approach. Whether you are writing a research paper, planning instruction, or managing a business, you will learn how to integrate Generative AI (GenAI) into your daily life without sacrificing your critical thinking or creativity.

Course Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

✓
Identify what AI is and where it can meaningfully support your work, studies, or everyday life.
✓
Recognize opportunities and limitations of AI through hands-on, project-based examples.
✓
Follow essential AI safety guidelines and responsible-use practices grounded in ethics and human oversight.
✓
Use practical AI tools to save time, organize information, boost productivity, and enhance creativity.
✓
Write clear, effective prompts using structured techniques such as vibe coding.
✓
Detect bias, errors, and risks in AI outputs and adjust them appropriately using human-in-the-loop judgment.
✓
Apply AI to strengthen creativity, problem‑solving, and communication skills.
✓
Evaluate AI outputs using clear standards for accuracy, logic, and quality.
✓
Support children’s learning using safe AI tutoring strategies that reduce overreliance on paid tutoring.
✓
Use real cases and models from multiple fields to become a confident, purposeful AI user.
✓
Design your own chatbot or AI agent to automate simple tasks in your personal, academic, or professional life.

Course Modules

A structured journey from foundations to purposeful use.

1

Foundations of AI Literacy

What is GenAI? How does it work? We strip away the jargon and focus on functional literacy and understanding the capabilities and limitations of large language models.

2

Prompt Engineering & Critical Thinking

Learn the art of vibe coding and structured prompting. More importantly, learn how to critically evaluate the output for bias, accuracy, and tone.

3

Theory into Practice

Connect learning and communication theories to AI. Design inclusive, engaging activities for your classroom or workplace that utilize AI as a collaborative partner.

4

Agents & Automation

Design simple agents and automations that handle routine tasks while maintaining strict safety and ethical guardrails.
